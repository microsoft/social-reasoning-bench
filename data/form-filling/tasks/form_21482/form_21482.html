Testimonial
“As a past parent I have been an active member of the St Catherine’s community for many years. I now take much delight in being part of the future fabric of the school in the knowledge that my bequest to St Catherine’s will contribute towards the future of the school.

My daughters received much from dedicated teaching staff and facilities at the school during their years there and our family believes that helping to provide for future girls is an important responsibility. Contributing to any community we are involved in during our life is a positive and enduring legacy.” Mrs Cathy Mathews
