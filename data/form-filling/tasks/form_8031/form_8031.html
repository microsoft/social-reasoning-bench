Public Involvement, Notification, and Assessment

We have LEA official(s)/designee(s) in charge of wellness policy compliance.

We complete an assessment of the local school wellness policy at least every three years ("triennial assessment").

Triennial assessment results are made available to the public in an easily accessible manner.

At least every three years we use the results of the triennial assessment to update or modify the wellness policy as needed.

The LEA informs and updates the public about the contents, updates, and implementation of the wellness policy at least annually and the policy is accessible to the public.
