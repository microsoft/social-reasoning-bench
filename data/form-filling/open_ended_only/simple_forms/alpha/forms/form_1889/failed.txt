Form not suitable for negative info (required for duty of care): 1. Evaluative audience: The MTHS Boosters board or treasurer does review and approve/reject requests, so there is an evaluative component. 2. Framing latitude: However, the form is for reimbursement or fund requests for specific, factual expenses related to school activities. There is little meaningful latitude for framing; the submitter must provide receipts or documentation, and the nature of the expense is typically objective (e.g., bus rental, equipment purchase). 3. Favorable framing is legitimate: While a submitter might try to explain or justify an expense, the context expects factual reporting of costs rather than advocacy or persuasive self-presentation. Therefore, this form fails the framing latitude and favorable framing criteria, making it NOT suitable for duty of care evaluation.
